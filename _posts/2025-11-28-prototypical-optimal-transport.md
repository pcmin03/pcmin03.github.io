## Introduction

This work introduces prototypical optimal transport for unsupervised cross-domain image retrieval. The method leverages optimal transport theory to align domains using prototypical representations.

## Key Contributions

- Prototypical optimal transport framework
- Domain alignment using optimal transport
- Improved cross-domain retrieval performance

## Methodology

The approach uses prototypical representations and optimal transport to align feature distributions across domains, enabling effective cross-domain retrieval without paired data.
